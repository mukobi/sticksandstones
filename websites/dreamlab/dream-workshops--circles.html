Your Dream Journey Starts Here
Dream Lab Workshops & Dream Circles are unforgettable experiences. Dream Workshops reveal the creative power and pure potential bursting through your sleep every night. And Dream Circles teach us about the collective unconscious and how it forms and informs us all.

The spirit of Dream Lab is one of discovery, exploration, community and knowledge sharing. Through dream workshops and dream circles you bring ancient wisdom to your present day experiences; you learn from the greatest minds to explore the unconscious realm, and from each other. Perhaps most importantly, you learn the language of your imagination, and enter into a lifelong relationship with your dreams.
